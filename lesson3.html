Culver's Team Member

Culver's Of Wake Forest

July 2021

My job is to help out with mostly everything. I chose to work then they have the least staff, so it can help them out, and I have more to do. I alternate between Drive Cashier, Custard, Set, Running, etc. Drive Cashier is simply cashing customers out in the Drive-Thru and sending them to park so their food can be brought to them. I am in charge of handing them their drinks and custard before giving them their number, after they pay. Custard is making the sundaes, shakes, smoothies, coolers, malts, concretes, etc. Set is gathering the food given from the kitchen and placing it all in a bag, along with napkins and condiments. Running is running the orders outside to the customers.
